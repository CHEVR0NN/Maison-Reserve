# WiBiz Agent Instructions

## Versioning & Changelog Discipline

1. Every deliverable carries a version from first draft (v1.0) and increments on every change (minor v1.0 → v1.1, major rewrite v1.0 → v2.0). This applies to everything: client dashboards, landing pages, handover docs, tech/build specs, SOPs, proposals, decks, scripts, sites, prompts.
2. Every build shipped to a client MUST version the dashboard AND the landing page, not just internal docs.
3. Keep a changelog for anything that evolves (what changed, when, by whom).
4. No unversioned deliverable is sent to a client or marked complete.
5. CC or BCC builds@wibiz.ai on ALL client email conversations so Pulse captures the thread. A conversation Pulse cannot see did not happen as far as the system is concerned.
